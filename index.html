About Us

Born from a love of bread and a passion for quality, we are a new bakery founded in 2021, specializing in French-inspired goods. In addition to chef-inspired cakes, pastries, signature coffee and tea, we offer a unique experience to thousands of guests daily. Today we have over 100 locations in Malaysia, satisfying cravings and taste buds of all ages and backgrounds.“ To be the highest quality craveable bakery, obsessed with our guest experience. ”
